Barangay Cleanup Drive

Residents unite to clean streets and canals

Residents of Barangay Marikina Heights participated in a community cleanup drive last Saturday morning. Volunteers worked together to remove trash from streets and canals.

The activity was organized by local officials to promote environmental awareness. Many students also joined the event to support a cleaner neighborhood.

New Public Library Opens

Students gain access to more learning resources

The Marikina City government recently opened a new public library for students and residents. The facility includes computer stations and study areas.

Officials hope the library will encourage young people to read more and improve their academic performance.
