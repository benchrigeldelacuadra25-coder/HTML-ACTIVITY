Quick Opinion

Many residents believe clean-up drives should happen monthly to keep our community spotless.

Barangay Clean-Up Drive Successful

Residents unite to protect the environment

Hundreds of volunteers joined the clean-up drive organized by the barangay council last Saturday morning. Residents worked together to remove trash from streets and canals.

The activity improved sanitation and raised environmental awareness. Officials say similar programs will continue to maintain cleanliness.

New Public Park Opens

Families now have a modern recreation area

The municipality opened a new park with jogging paths, benches, playgrounds, and open spaces for events.

Residents say the park provides a safe place for families to gather and enjoy outdoor activities.

School Facilities Upgraded

Better learning environment for students

Local schools received digital boards, new computers, and upgraded classrooms to improve the learning experience.

Teachers and students are excited about the improvements and hope it motivates better academic performance.

Barangay Health Center Hosts Free Checkups

Community prioritizes health and wellness

The local health center provided free medical checkups and consultations to residents last weekend.

Doctors emphasized preventive health care and encouraged residents to maintain a healthy lifestyle.

Youth Sports Festival Kicks Off

Young athletes show their talents

The barangay sports festival featured basketball, volleyball, and athletics competitions among the youth.

Organizers hope the event inspires more young people to join sports programs and lead active lifestyles.
