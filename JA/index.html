City Launches Flood Control Program

The Marikina City government has launched a new flood control program to reduce flooding in low-lying areas. The project includes drainage improvement and river cleanup.

Officials say the program will help protect thousands of families during the rainy season. Community volunteers are also encouraged to participate in river cleanup activities.
