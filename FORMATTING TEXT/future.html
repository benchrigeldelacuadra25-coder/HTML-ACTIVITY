Our Community in 2035

By 2035, our community could become a smart and connected city. Schools may adopt smart classrooms with digital boards, tablets, and interactive lessons that improve student learning.

Transportation may become more efficient with improved roads, organized public transport, and bike lanes. These improvements could reduce traffic congestion and make travel easier.

Digital government services may also allow residents to access documents, pay taxes, and request services online. With innovation and cooperation, our community can become more modern and progressive.
