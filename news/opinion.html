Why Community Cleanliness Matters

A clean community is important for health and safety. When streets and canals are full of trash, it can cause flooding and spread diseases.

Residents and local officials should work together to keep the community clean. Schools can also teach students about proper waste disposal and environmental responsibility.

If everyone participates in small actions like throwing trash properly and joining cleanup drives, the whole community will benefit.
